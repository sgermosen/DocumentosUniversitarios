On Valuation with Microsoft® Excel

In recent years, financial mathematics theory has grown in very important ways. This means that a huge variety of new asset valuation methods have been developed so as to improve on some older ones. On valuation is a book that focuses on the methods that are usually applied in the financial world, including both classical and new models. The reader will be able to valuate, among others, equities, bonds, options and investment projects.

The book is appropriate for any practitioner or [manager corporation] who wants to value assets in order to have the information to make an investment decision. It is also appropriate for introductory academic courses in finance, that are based on the practical aspects of asset valuation.

The topics are treated in a very straightforward way that allows the reader to develop a clear understanding of each valuation method. The book can be read in two ways: straight through from the first page to the last or topic by topic. The reader can study the whole book to get a complete overview of the way in which the asset valuations are made; or choose a single topic so that a direct asset valuation can be implemented.

On valuation comes with a CD that includes all the asset valuation models that are developed in the book. This CD demonstrates the easy application of these important financial tools.

 

Ingeniera Financiera. Futuros y Opciones con Microsoft® Excel

El objetivo del presente libro es brindarles, a los profesionales del Mercado Financiero y a sus clientes, un conjunto de conocimientos teóricos y prácticos necesarios para operar en los -cada vez más desarrollados- mercados de derivados financieros.  Con este fin se presentan soluciones en Microsoft® Excel que facilitan la comprensión de la temática desarrollada, permitiendo al lector utilizarlas a efectos de comprender y aplicar los conceptos explicados en su tarea diaria.

La primera sección de Ingeniería Financiera. Futuros y Opciones describe los mercados conocidos como futuros y los contratos a término. La siguiente sección nos introduce en la teoría de opciones. La última parte utiliza los conocimientos presentados anteriormente para realizar coberturas de diferentes riesgos financieros.
